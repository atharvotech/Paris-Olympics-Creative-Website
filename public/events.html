Historical Records
Usain Bolt
Sport: Athletics (Track and Field)
Story: Usain Bolt holds the Olympic records for the 100m (9.63 seconds, London 2012), 200m (19.30 seconds, Beijing 2008), and 4x100m relay (36.84 seconds, London 2012) with the Jamaican team.
Bob Beamon
Sport: Athletics (Long Jump)
Story: Bob Beamon set a long jump record of 8.90 meters at the Mexico City 1968 Olympics, a record that stood for 23 years and is one of the most famous moments in Olympic history.
Wayde van Niekerk
Sport: Athletics (400m)
Story: Wayde van Niekerk broke the world record in the 400m with a time of 43.03 seconds at the Rio 2016 Olympics, surpassing the previous record set by Michael Johnson.
Michael Phelps
Sport: Swimming
Story: Michael Phelps won 8 gold medals at the Beijing 2008 Olympics, the most gold medals in a single Olympics. He is the most decorated Olympian of all time with a total of 23 gold medals.
Katie Ledecky
Sport: Swimming (800m Freestyle)
Story: Katie Ledecky set the Olympic record in the 800m freestyle with a time of 8:04.79 at the Rio 2016 Olympics. She is known for her dominance in distance swimming.
Nadia Comăneci
Sport: Gymnastics
Story: Nadia Comăneci became the first gymnast to score a perfect 10 in an Olympic event at the Montreal 1976 Olympics. She achieved this feat at the age of 14.
Naim Süleymanoğlu
Sport: Weightlifting
Story: Naim Süleymanoğlu, known as the "Pocket Hercules," won gold medals in three different Olympics (1988, 1992, 1996) and set multiple world records.
Eliud Kipchoge
Sport: Marathon
Story: Eliud Kipchoge set the Olympic record in the marathon with a time of 2:08:38 at the Rio 2016 Olympics. He is widely regarded as the greatest marathon runner of all time.
