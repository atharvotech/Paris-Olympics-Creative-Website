Sports

The Paris 2024 Olympics featured a diverse array of sports, including both traditional events and some exciting new additions. Here are the details: Returning Sports:

The Paris 2024 Olympics included 28 returning sports, such as:

  • Athletics:Track and field events, marathons, and race walking.

  • Swimming: Various styles including freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly, along with diving and water polo.

  • Gymnastics: Artistic, rhythmic, and trampoline gymnastics.

  • Basketball: Both 5-on-5 and 3x3 formats.

  • Football (Soccer): Men’s and women’s tournaments.

  • Tennis: Singles and doubles events for both men and women.

  • Cycling: Road cycling, track cycling, mountain biking, and BMX1.

Indian Athletes at Paris 2024 Olympics



A total of 117 Indian athletes have competed in the Paris 2024 Olympics, participating in 16 sports disciplines. Here are some notable athletes and their achievements:



  • Neeraj Chopra: The javelin thrower who won a silver medal, becoming the most successful individual Olympian from India.


  • Manu Bhaker: She made history by winning two bronze medals in shooting, including the mixed team 10m air pistol event with Sarabjot Singh.


  • Aman Sehrawat: The youngest Indian Olympic medallist, winning bronze in wrestling.


  • Indian Men’s Hockey Team: They matched their Tokyo 2020 success by winning a bronze medal.
